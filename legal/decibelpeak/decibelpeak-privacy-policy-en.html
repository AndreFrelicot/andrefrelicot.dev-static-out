2.1. No Personal Data Collection: The Developer explicitly states that no personal data is collected, stored, processed, or transmitted by this Application.

3. AUDIO PROCESSING

3.1. Real-Time Processing Only: The Application accesses the device microphone solely for real-time sound level measurement purposes.

3.2. Audio data is processed according to the following principles:

  1. Audio streams are processed immediately upon receipt;
  2. No audio data is recorded, stored, or cached;
  3. No audio data is transmitted to any external servers or services;
  4. Audio data is discarded immediately after decibel calculation;
  5. No persistent storage of audio-related information occurs.
