Summary: BLE UScope does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data whatsoever. This application operates entirely on your device and respects your privacy completely.

1. Introduction

André Frélicot ("we," "our," or "us") operates the BLE UScope mobile application for iOS and Android platforms (the "Application"). This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with your use of the Application.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains that we do not collect, use, store, or share any personal information or data from users of the Application.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Application does not collect, transmit, store, or process any information about you, including but not limited to:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number, etc.)
  • Device identifiers or advertising identifiers
  • Location data or geolocation information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Browsing history or search queries
  • Bluetooth device information beyond local device operation
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data
  • Any other personal or non-personal information

3. How the Application Works

The Application operates entirely on your local device and performs the following functions:

  • Bluetooth Scanning: The Application scans for nearby Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) devices using your device's Bluetooth hardware. All scanning and analysis occurs locally on your device.
  • Device Analysis: Any BLE device information discovered is processed and displayed solely within the Application on your device.
  • Local Storage: User preferences, favorite devices, and settings are stored locally on your device using iOS or Android local storage mechanisms. This data never leaves your device.
  • No Internet Connection Required: The Application does not require an internet connection to function and does not transmit any data over the internet.

4. Bluetooth Permissions

The Application requires Bluetooth permissions to scan for and connect to Bluetooth Low Energy devices. These permissions are used exclusively for the core functionality of the Application:

  • Scanning for nearby BLE devices
  • Connecting to BLE devices
  • Reading and writing BLE characteristics
  • Monitoring BLE device status

All Bluetooth operations are performed locally on your device. No information about discovered devices or Bluetooth activity is transmitted to us or any third party.

5. Third-Party Services

The Application does not integrate with, utilize, or transmit data to any third-party services, including:

  • Social media platforms
  • Any other third-party service providers
